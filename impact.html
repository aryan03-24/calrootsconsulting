THE ROOTS IMPACT FUND

Supporting Our Communities One Step Further

The Roots Impact Fund was created to deepen our commitment to supporting small businesses and go beyond advisory support by directly reinvesting in the communities we serve. As a fully pro bono student consulting organization, Roots Consulting provides strategic support at no cost to the businesses and nonprofits we work with.

Contributions to the Impact Fund help finance initiatives such as small business micro-grants, marketing and operational resources for local entrepreneurs, community programming, and tools that enable our student teams to deliver high-quality strategic work.

Every dollar raised through the Impact Fund is dedicated to strengthening local businesses, expanding access to resources, and driving long-term community impact. By supporting the fund, you are helping empower entrepreneurs, amplify local stories, and build a more resilient small business ecosystem.

How To Donate

To contribute to the Roots Impact Fund, please use our official donation portal.

  1. Visit the donation site: asuc.org/donate
  2. In the designation or organization field, enter: Roots Consulting
  3. Complete the donation form with your preferred contribution amount.

All donations are tax-deductible as Roots Consulting operates as a registered student organization under the University of California, Berkeley.
