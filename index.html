WHAT IS ROOTS?

Founded in 2025 at the University of California, Berkeley by Berkeley Haas transfer students, Roots Consulting is UC Berkeley's premier pro-bono advisory association for small businesses, committed to helping local businesses grow sustainably, innovate boldly, and thrive in a rapidly changing world.

For Our Clients & Partners

We partner with local small businesses and community organizations to deliver practical, data-driven solutions that support sustainable growth. Through close collaboration and a deep understanding of each client's goals, we help strengthen operations, increase visibility, and create lasting, meaningful progress.

For Students

We provide hands-on consulting experience through real client projects that develop both technical and leadership skills. Members gain mentorship, teamwork experience, and professional exposure while making a tangible impact in the communities they serve.
